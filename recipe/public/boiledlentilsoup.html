How to cook boiled lentil soup

Lentils are a popular staple in the South Asian continent, and are a good source of protein, zinc, magnesium, and other nutrients. This is a simple yet nutritious recipe that can be served as part of a meal.

1.Wash 1-3 cups of lentils. and add to a water-filled pot.

2.Chop 5-10 small to medium chopped Serrano peppers and 1-2 small onions, and acquire 1 teaspoon of ground ginger paste, 1 teaspoon of coriander leaves, 2 teaspoons of salt, and 1-2 bay leaves.

3.Boil the lentils and add the aforementioned materials, as well as 1 teaspoon of red pepper,1 teaspoon of tumeric (for coloring). Chopped tomatoes and other fruits and vegetables are optional. Stir thouroughly.

4.Cook the lentil mix until it is liquefied.
