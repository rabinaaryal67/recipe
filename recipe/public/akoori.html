How to cook a Indian akoori (scrambled egg)

How to cook a egg akoori

1.Crack 3-4 eggs and add them to a bowl. Whisk heavily, add 1/4th a cup of milk for creamy texture, and 2 teaspoons of salt

2.Acquire a medium size pan to place on the stove and add a teaspoon of vegetable oil.

3.Chop one half of an onion, 2 Serrano peppers, and crush one garlic clove. Add to the pan and let it fry for 1 minute.

4. Chop one tomato and add to the mixture to cook for 1-2 minutes.

5. Add the egg mixture into the pan, and 1 teaspoon each of black pepper, coriander leaves, and crushed red pepper, as well as 1/4 garam masala, cumin, tumeric, and red pepper powder.

6. Cover the pan and scramble from time to time for 5-10 minutes. Ensure both sides are properly cooked. When finished, eat with a piece of bread.
