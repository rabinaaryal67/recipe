How to create chicken samosas

Samosas are a popular fried snack in which a filling is fried in a wrapped and rolled sheet of grain, and here is how we make it.

1.Acquire several frozen samosa sheets and leave them out to thaw.

2.Marinate chicken pieces in turmeric, red/black pepper, cumin, coriander,salt, and other spices. Cook for a few minutes in water, then drain the water, and cut into small pieces

3.Cook chopped onion pieces with garlic and ginger paste in vegetable oil, add chopped potato pieces and chopped Serrano peppers, When mixture is brownish, add the chicken and stir in low cooking heat.

4. Make a paste by mixing flour and water. For each samosa, add the cooked filling and fold sheet into triangle and glue using paste.

5.Keep samosas frozen until you want to eat them, if you wish to eat them, deep fry them with vegetable oil.
