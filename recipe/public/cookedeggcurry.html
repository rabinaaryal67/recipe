How to cook a Indian non-boiled egg curry

A delicious South Asian dish that mixes spicy curry with tenderly cooked eggs

1.Peel and chop 2 onions (4 pieces each) and add to a processor/blender to grind.

2.Acquire a pan, wash it and place on the stove to dry with 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil.

3.Add onion paste (1-2 teaspoons), 2 teaspoons of salt, and add to the pan and fry.

4. When onion turns brown, add water, red pepper, tumeric, brown coriander, and (optionally) a chopped garlic.

4. Mix the mixture and cover the pan for 5 minutes

5. Take out six eggs, mix the curry further, and pour a full water cup into the pan. Break each egg in a bowl at a time and pour into the pan. Cover the pan and increase heat for 10 minutes.

6.Chop 6 scallion leaves and 3 Serrano peppers and add to the mix. Also add one spoon of coriander leaves and one spoon of curry powder. Cook for 5 more minutes.
